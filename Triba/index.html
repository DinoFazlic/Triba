TRIBA

The game consists of a grid of m×n points, where m and n are even natural numbers. Two players take turns playing alternately. On each turn, a player selects a triangle by choosing three points from the grid that do not lie on the same straight line. The triangles must not intersect, and no two triangles can share a vertex. Triangles can be nested within one another. The game ends when a player can no longer make a move. The player who is unable to make a move loses. In the diagram below, the side lines represent the color of the player whose turn it is.
